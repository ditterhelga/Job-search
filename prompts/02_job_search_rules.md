# Job Search Rules (Active Search)

## Goal

Find the best opportunities with the highest probability of getting interviews in the next few months.

Optimize for interview probability, not prestige.

## Search mode

Current mode: ACTIVE_SEARCH.

The bot runs only manually when Olga starts a search session.

No scheduled notifications.

## Roles

High priority:

- Product Designer
- Senior Product Designer
- UX Designer
- UX/UI Designer
- Digital Product Designer
- Interaction Designer
- Experience Designer

Reject immediately:

- Lead Product Designer
- Staff Product Designer
- Principal Product Designer
- Head of Design
- Design Manager
- Product Design Manager
- Junior
- Graduate
- Intern

Also reject:

- Graphic Designer
- Brand Designer
- Marketing Designer
- Motion Designer
- Illustrator
- UI Artist
- Content Designer
- Social Media Designer

## Location

Netherlands:

Accept remote, hybrid, and on-site.

Outside Netherlands:

Accept only remote.

Priority countries:

- Germany
- Belgium
- France
- Spain
- Portugal
- Austria
- Switzerland
- Denmark
- Sweden
- Finland
- Ireland
- UAE / Dubai

Also accept:

- Remote EU
- Remote EMEA
- Worldwide Remote

Reject:

- Relocation required
- Hybrid outside Netherlands
- On-site outside Netherlands
- US only
- Canada only
- UK resident only
- Dutch required

## Domains

Domain is a weak filter.

High priority:

- Enterprise SaaS
- Internal Tools
- AI
- Fintech
- Payments
- Logistics
- Operations
- Healthcare
- EdTech
- HR Tech
- Travel
- Productivity
- Marketplaces
- Developer Tools
- E-commerce
- B2B
- B2C

Lower priority:

- Consumer products
- Agencies

Reject only if primarily branding, marketing, advertising, illustration, social media, visual-only, or web-only work.

## Telegram output

For every recommended vacancy provide:

- Company
- Role
- Location
- Short summary of what the company/product is
- Green flags
- Red flags
- Best case study to mention
- Interview chance 0–100%
- Recommended action

Actions:

- Apply immediately
- Apply today
- Save for later
- Skip

## Learning

After every recommendation Olga can answer:

- 👍 job_id = relevant
- 👎 job_id = not relevant

Store every response.

Use this history to improve future recommendations.

Never overwrite explicit rules, only adjust ranking.

## Philosophy

It is better to send three good vacancies than thirty average ones.

Do not recommend jobs only because the title matches.

Recommend jobs that Olga has a realistic chance of interviewing for now.
